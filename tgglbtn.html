Flight Service Stations have discontinued Airport Advisory services within the Continental U.S., Puerto Rico, and Hawaii, due to declining demand and pilot requests. Therefore, we have removed references to Remote Airport Advisory service and Local Airport Advisory service from FAA directives. Airport Advisory services in Alaska remain unchanged. This change reflects the migration of En Route Flight Advisory Service responsibilities into the Inflight position and the discontinued use of the term “Flight Watch” within the Continental U.S. and Puerto Rico. The paragraphs within chapter 7 have also been updated due to changes in Advisory Circular 00-45H, Aviation Weather Services.
